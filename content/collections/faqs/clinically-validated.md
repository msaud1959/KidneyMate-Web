title: Is KidneyMate clinically validated?
answer: Not yet, and we won't pretend otherwise. The framework behind KidneyMate is grounded in the published literature and is currently under peer review, and the product has been shaped through around 50 stakeholder conversations. Formal clinical validation research is planned as the next major milestone, and we make no outcome claims ahead of it.
