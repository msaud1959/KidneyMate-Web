title: What is KidneyMate?
answer: KidneyMate is a digital health platform in development for people living with chronic kidney disease. It brings daily self-management into one place (fluids, food, medications, symptoms, sleep, mood, appointments and dialysis sessions), turns those records into plain-language insights, and, with your consent, into structured summaries and review flags your care team can act on. It's built on a research framework, currently under peer review, in which clinicians review anything that matters.
