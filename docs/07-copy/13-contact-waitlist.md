## 4. What happens next [numbered_steps]

**Heading:** What joining actually means

1. **A confirmation lands in your inbox.** So you know it worked, and so you have our address.
2. **Updates when there's something real.** A milestone, a pilot opening, a release. If a month is quiet, so are we.
3. **Early access, in signup order.** When the app opens up, invitations go out down the list, oldest first.
4. **For clinicians and researchers: a conversation.** If you've ticked pilot or research interest, we'll reach out personally to start the discussion.
