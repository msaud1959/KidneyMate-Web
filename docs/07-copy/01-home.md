## 4. The daily reality [split_content + stat_band]

**Heading:** Living with kidney disease is daily work nobody applied for

**Body:**
How much can I drink today, in this heat? Is there too much potassium in that? Did I take the morning tablets, or am I remembering yesterday? Why am I this tired?

For millions of people, these questions repeat every day, between appointments that might be months apart. The tracking lands in notebooks, scattered apps and memory. Most of it never reaches the people responsible for their care.

**Link:** Read about the problem →

**Stat band:**
- **2.7 million**
  Australian adults showing signs of kidney disease, about 1 in 7. Most don't know it.
  Source: Australian Bureau of Statistics, 2022 to 2024
- **788 million**
  Adults worldwide living with chronic kidney disease in 2023, more than double the 1990 figure.
  Source: Global Burden of Disease study
- **17% to 74%**
  The reported range of people with CKD who struggle to keep up with medication and diet plans.
  Source: published adherence studies

## 5. What KidneyMate does [pillar_trio]

**Heading:** One steady place, three quiet jobs

**Pillar 1: Track your day**
Fluids against your personal limit. Meals and the things worth watching. Medications with gentle reminders. Symptoms, sleep, mood, appointments and dialysis sessions. A minute here, a minute there, all in one place.

**Pillar 2: Understand your patterns**
KidneyMate compares your records with your own usual pattern and the targets your care team sets, then tells you what it notices in plain language. Every insight can show its working. No black boxes, no alarm bells.

**Pillar 3: Stay connected to care**
When you choose to share, your records become clear summaries and review flags for your clinician: the story of your weeks, not a guess from memory. People make the decisions. KidneyMate just makes sure they can see.

## 6. How it works, briefly [layered_framework]

**Heading:** No black boxes. Three honest layers.

**Layer 1: You record your day.** Small entries, made easy: what you drank, what you took, how you slept, how you feel.

**Layer 2: KidneyMate makes sense of it.** Clear, explainable rules look for trends and changes against your own baseline, never a stranger's average.

**Layer 3: Your care team reviews what matters.** Clinicians see organised summaries and flags, add their judgement, and decide what happens next. Always people, never software alone.

**Footnote:** This is the framework from our peer-reviewed research, built into the product from day one.
