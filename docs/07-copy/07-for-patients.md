## 2. Is KidneyMate for you? [icon_points]

**Heading:** Made for the many shapes of kidney disease

- **Recently diagnosed.** The learning curve is steep and the advice comes fast. KidneyMate gives the new routine somewhere to live from day one.
- **Living with CKD long-term.** When the routine is old but heavy, KidneyMate carries the tracking and shows you your patterns, not just your slips.
- **On dialysis.** Sessions, fluid limits between them, symptom swings: the parts of dialysis life that happen outside the chair get a place to be seen.
- **Caring for someone with CKD.** Partners, kids, parents: carers do invisible work too. KidneyMate helps you stay usefully close (more below).

## 3. What your day could feel like [before_after]

**Heading:** The same Tuesday, twice

**Before: the juggle**
Fluid count in your head, lost by lunch. A photo of the new medication schedule somewhere in your camera roll. That symptom you meant to mention, gone by the time the appointment comes. And a nagging feeling you're forgetting something, because you usually are.

**After: the routine**
Taps instead of tallies. Reminders that carry the remembering. A gentle note when something's drifting, with the reason one tap away. And come appointment day, months of your real story, organised, in your hand. Same condition. Different load.

## 4. Designed for real life [icon_points]

**Heading:** The details that make it liveable

- **Seconds, not sessions.** Most entries are one or two taps. No essays, no forms with twelve fields.
- **Easy on the eyes.** Big type, clear contrast, calm colours. Designed for tired eyes and every age of hands.
- **No guilt, ever.** Missed a day? A week? KidneyMate doesn't scold, reset your worth, or send passive-aggressive streak messages. It just quietly carries on with you.
- **Start with one thing.** Use only the fluid tracker for a month if you like. Every tracker stands alone; the picture just gets richer as you add.
- **Your pace on sharing.** Nothing goes to anyone unless you choose it. Private diary first, shared summary only if and when you say so.

---

## 5. You stay in control [icon_points]

**Heading:** Yours, in every sense

- **Your records belong to you.** Export them whenever you want. Delete them and they're gone.
- **You choose who sees what.** Sharing with your care team is opt-in, visible and reversible.
- **The core app will be free for patients.** The essentials of daily self-management shouldn't have a paywall in front of them.
- **No ads, no data sales.** Not now, not ever. It's in our privacy commitments in writing.

---

## 6. For carers [rich_prose]

**Heading:** For the people who carry it alongside

If you help someone live with kidney disease, you already know the strange double role: close enough to worry, not always close enough to know. Nagging feels awful. Not asking feels worse.

KidneyMate is designed to give that worry somewhere better to go. With the patient's consent, a carer can help keep records complete, see how the routine's really going, and know the care team can see what matters. Less interrogation over dinner, more actual dinner.
