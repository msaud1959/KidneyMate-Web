# Form email wording

Ready-to-use, claims-safe wording for the waitlist and contact forms. All of
it keeps to the site's rules: no medical advice or clinical claims, the
framework is described as "currently under peer review", Australian English,
and no promises about final pricing.

---

## A. Alerts you receive (set these up in Netlify)

In Netlify: **Forms → your form → Settings and notifications → Add
notification → Email notification**, send to `msaud1959@gmail.com`.
Netlify includes the submitted fields automatically; you only choose a subject.

- **Waitlist form subject:** `New KidneyMate waitlist signup`
- **Contact form subject:** `New KidneyMate contact message`

---

## B. Confirmation to the person (waitlist)

Netlify's free plan does not auto-reply to the sender, so use this either as a
manual reply, or wire it through a free autoresponder later (for example a
Zapier or Make step triggered by the Netlify submission). Replace `[First name]`.

**Subject:** You're on the KidneyMate waitlist

> Hi [First name],
>
> Thanks for joining the KidneyMate waitlist. You're in.
>
> KidneyMate is in development now, built on research and shaped by
> conversations with people living with kidney disease and the clinicians who
> care for them. We will send you honest updates only when something real
> happens, and when early access opens, invitations go out in signup order.
>
> One thing we take seriously: KidneyMate supports self-management and does not
> provide medical advice, diagnosis or treatment. Please keep following the
> guidance of your own care team, and in an emergency call 000.
>
> You can leave the list at any time by replying to this email.
>
> Warm regards,
> The KidneyMate team

## C. Acknowledgement to the person (contact)

**Subject:** We have received your message

> Hi [Name],
>
> Thanks for getting in touch with KidneyMate. A real person reads every
> message, and we usually reply within a few business days.
>
> KidneyMate supports self-management and does not provide medical advice,
> diagnosis or treatment. If your message is about your own health or care,
> please speak with your care team, and in an emergency call 000.
>
> Warm regards,
> The KidneyMate team
